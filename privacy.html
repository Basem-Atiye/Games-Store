Overview

GameNova is a demo static storefront created to showcase a modern, responsive UI. This policy explains what the demo stores in your browser and what choices you have.

Data we store locally

This demo stores minimal data in your browser (localStorage) only:

  • Cart — kept under gamenova_cart to persist items between visits.
  • Wishlist — stored under gamenova_wl.
  • Contact submissions — saved under gamenova_contacts if you use the contact form (client-side only).

No server-side tracking

This demo does not send analytics, telemetry, or other personal data to a backend. If you integrate third-party services later, those services may collect data .

Your choices

You can clear local data anytime via browser settings or developer tools. Clearing site storage will remove cart, wishlist and saved messages.

Contact

If you have questions about this demo's privacy practices, use the contact page.
